Water and Waste Water Treatment Plant

Our Water and Wastewater Treatment Plant combines cutting-edge technology with expert processes to deliver high-quality water and efficient wastewater management.

Water treatment process begins with the intake of raw water, where we employ coagulation and flocculation to remove impurities, followed by sedimentation to allow larger particles to settle.
The resulting clarified water is then subjected to advanced multi-stage filtration and disinfection techniques, including chlorination and UV treatment, ensuring that it meets the highest safety standards for public consumption.
In our wastewater treatment operations, we focus on protecting the environment while effectively processing sewage and industrial effluents.
Our comprehensive approach includes preliminary screening to eliminate large solids, primary sedimentation to separate sludge, and advanced biological treatment methods such as activated sludge processes to break down organic matter.

Railway Tunnel Ventilation Systems

Railway tunnel ventilation systems play a crucial role in ensuring passenger safety and mitigating the spread of fire during emergencies.

These systems are designed to control smoke, heat, and toxic gases, providing a safe environment for evacuation and emergency response.
This system monitors the tunnel environment to ensure optimal conditions for train operations and passenger safety and it is used to monitor the air quality within a tunnel.
If the CO2/NO2/NO/CO levels exceed a predefined threshold, the system can automatically adjust ventilation rates to improve air circulation and ensure passenger comfort.
There are designated safe areas within the tunnel where passengers can seek shelter during emergencies.
