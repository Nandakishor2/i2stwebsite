Company Info

History

I2S Technologies Pvt. Limited, headquartered at Bangalore, India started by technocrats with a vision to provide solutions with state of art technologies and methodologies for control & instrumentation, electrical and safety requirements, with an enthusiasm for bringing smarter environs.

I2S Technologies was conceived as a technological startup to deliver critical infrastructure projects and cutting edge & advanced engineering solutions. The core of the company is to develop specialization in the system integration business, by providing highest grade automation and information systems, with standardization and ease of use to customers. I2ST is a Security and Safety company providing solutions for integrated buildings and industry alike with adhering to international standards for the infrastructure projects.

Mr. Aarun Thapliyal, the CEO of I2S Technologies, brings over three decades of industry experience, guiding the company with a forward-thinking approach. His passion for innovation in technologies and commitment to excellence have been instrumental in positioning I2ST as a leader in Mass Transportation segment .
Under his leadership, I2ST continues to thrive by delivering cutting-edge, efficient solutions to its clients.
